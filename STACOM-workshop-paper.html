STACOM workshop paper
Participants are invited to submit an 8 pages manuscript describing the algorithm and results of the training and validation phase for consideration in the MICCAI STACOM workshop 2022. Accepted challenge papers will be published in Lecture Notes in Computer Science proceeding published by Springer (see previous STACOM proceedings).
General rules for the paper submission:

  1. Please Follow the LNCS template from Springer for your manuscript. Initial submission must be anonymized and limited to 8 pages (text, tables, and figures) + up to 2 pages for references.
  2. All papers will undergo a peer review process. Submit your paper through the Springer submission system (https://equinocs.springernature.com/service/STACOM2022) and make sure you choose the “CMRxMotion” category.
  3. A paper placeholder must be submitted before 15 July 2023.
  4. Please note that your manuscript will be considered for publication only if you successfully submit the docker container.
