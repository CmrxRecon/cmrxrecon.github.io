Q&A: 

Q: What is the estimated time for approval of participation request after sending the signed challenge rule?
A: If the signed challenge rule document is filled out correctly, the participation request will be approved within 2-4 working days.

Q: Is it possible to download the challenge dataset without participating in the challenge? 
A: No, the dataset is only available to participants during the challenge to ensure enough submissions.

Q: What is the maximum number of team members allowed? 
A: Each team can consist of up to 6 people. The authors in the submitted paper should be the same as the team member list.

Q: Can other datasets or pre-trained models be used to develop the segmentation algorithms? 
A: No, only the challenge dataset is allowed for developing the segmentation algorithms.

Q: Is manual annotation of unlabeled images allowed to increase the training set? 
A: No, any manual interventions are not allowed, including manual annotation of unlabeled images.

Q: Can high-quality pseudo labels for the unlabeled images be manually selected? 
A: No, any manual interventions are not allowed, including manual selection of high-quality pseudo labels for unlabeled images.

Q: Does the validation submission affect the final ranking? 
A: No, the validation submission does not affect the final ranking.

Q: Can modifications be made to the methods and the paper during the testing phase? 
A: Yes, modifications can be made before the testing submission, but not after.
